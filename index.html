Samba path converter

Upload a full link for an asset on mcasset.cloud. EX: https://mcasset.cloud/1.20.1/assets/minecraft/textures/block/amethyst_block.png

Output will appear here

from js import document def handle(*args): input_element = document.getElementById("user-input") txt = input_element.value txt = txt.replace("https://mcasset.cloud/1.20.1/","") txt = txt.replace("/","\\") document.getElementById("output").textContent = txt
